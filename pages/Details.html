Details of Our Special Day

Attire

For those who will be joining us for the rehearsal dinner we suggest the following attire:

Men- Dress slacks and button-down or polo shirt and a sports coat or blazer is optional.

Women- sun dress, skirt or pants with a blouse.

On the big day the ceremony and reception will be held outside. For our guest who are not familiar with the VA climate the average high for our wedding day is 70 and the low is 48. The only thing we ask of our guest is to dress according to the forecast. We suggest the following attire: Men- Coat and tie or suit. Women-Cocktail dress or a dressy skirt and top.
